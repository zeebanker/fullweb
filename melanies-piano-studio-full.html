About the Studio

Meet your teacher and learn about our approach

🎹

Hello, I'm Melanie

Welcome to my studio! I've been teaching piano in the Eden Prairie area for over 15 years, and nothing brings me more joy than watching students discover their musical potential.

My teaching philosophy centers on building a strong classical foundation while keeping lessons engaging and fun. I believe every student—whether 5 or 75—can experience the deep satisfaction of making music.

When I'm not at the piano, you'll find me hiking Minnesota's beautiful trails, exploring local coffee shops, or attending concerts at Orchestra Hall.

Credentials & Training

  • Bachelor of Music, Piano Performance
  • 15+ years teaching experience
  • Member, Music Teachers National Association
  • Certified in Suzuki Method (Books 1-4)
  • Regular workshop presenter and adjudicator

Studio Policies

Guidelines to help our studio run smoothly

📅 Scheduling

Lessons are scheduled weekly. Use the student portal to request reschedules with 24 hours notice.

💳 Payment

Tuition due on the first lesson of each month. Check, Venmo, or Zelle accepted.

🚫 Cancellations

Less than 24 hours notice cannot be made up. Teacher cancellations always rescheduled.

🎹 Practice

Regular practice is essential. Aim for 5-6 days per week at recommended duration.
